Hey everyone!

Thank you for having an interest in my portfolio. My name is Caitlyn Rijks. I’m 20 years old and a senior year Game-Art student at Mediacollege Amsterdam.

In addition to my studies, I work as a Junior artist at Azerion, where I started as an intern. The team and I clicked so well that they loved me to stay as their colleague at the casual studio. I will be working there full time after my study and I’m looking forward to that!

As an artist my main goal is to be inspired by the people around me and to inspire others. I loved drawing since the day I could hold a pencil. I like to visualize stories. So when I found out I could make it my job, I didn’t want anything else. I’ve grown so much over the past few years and I can’t wait for all the amazing new experiences that may come my way. I like making games that make people happy and relaxed. Besides making game art, I also make illustrations.

I am a big fan of teamwork, so if you would like to collaborate, don’t hesitate to contact me.
